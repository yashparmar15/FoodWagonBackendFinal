Know About Us

Overview

Founded in 2019 by Rajdeep Gautam and Mridul Sharma in a home office of Mathura and evolved from small business of battery operating foodcart, functioning particularly to shift towards online catering and foodmarts. We now help vendors all over India, help them to range their small food business to large scale and are thrilled to be a part of this rapidly changing world to revolutionize the concept of street food on food carts. Currently we have effectively adapted ourselves in the era of globalization where there is continuous demand of people to shift from normal pushcart to FOODWAGON’s battery operated foodcart. We’re dedicated to provide you the best experience of our products, with assured loyalty, uniqueness and fulfilling customer experience. We hope you enjoy our products and services as much as we enjoy offering it to you.
